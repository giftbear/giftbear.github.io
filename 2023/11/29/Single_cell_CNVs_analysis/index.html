Usage

  1. How to get gene position file?
    • Download gtf_to_position_file.py: https://github.com/broadinstitute/infercnv/blob/master/scripts/gtf_to_position_file.py
    • python3 gtf_to_position_file.py --attribute_name "gene_name" /opt/refdata-gex-GRCh38-2020-A/genes/genes.gtf gene_position.txt
