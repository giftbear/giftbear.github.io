参考基因组和注释文件对应版本下载
2023-04-07|Omics

以下均以人Hg38(GRCh38)版本为例

Ensembl

  • https://asia.ensembl.org/Homo_sapiens/Info/Index
  • Fasta: Homo_sapiens.GRCh38.dna.primary_assembly.fa.gz (https://ftp.ensembl.org/pub/release-109/fasta/homo_sapiens/dna/)
  • GTF: Homo_sapiens.GRCh38.109.chr.gtf.gz (https://ftp.ensembl.org/pub/release-109/gtf/homo_sapiens/)

Gencode

  • https://www.gencodegenes.org/human/
  • Fasta: Genome sequence (GRCh38.p13) + ALL
  • GTF: Comprehensive gene annotation + CHR

NCBI

  • https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/projects/genome/guide/human/index.shtml

UCSC

  • Fasta: hg38.fa.gz (https://hgdownload.soe.ucsc.edu/goldenPath/hg38/bigZips/)
  • GTF: http://genome.ucsc.edu/cgi-bin/hgTables
